Tram depots are big, unattractive areas, which need to be placed quite central in cities. Their service is crucial for tram networks to operate, but they don't offer any public functions for citizens. Can we reevaluate their potential and offer additional uses?

This project develops an exhibition space over a tramway station on the riverbank of the Limmat in Zurich. A main focus was on the structural design, to make the exhibition space essentially fly over the tram tracks. The suggestion is to hang the exhibition platform from a big triangulated load-bearing shell. The shell serves as a structural, weather-resistant layer, but still provides plenty of light for the exhibition.
