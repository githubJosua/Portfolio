CAN WE CAST THE MOTION OF GLACIERS INTO SEDIMENT?

// GLACIAL REMNANTS //

ETH ZÜRICH - STUDIO ANNE HOLTROP

This Project seeks to preserve the vanishing but rich geometries from glacial surfaces in something more permanent. Since one of the most defining processes of glacial surface creation is erosion through meltwater, this project lets big ice blocks be eroded and formed by water. The ice will further be used as formwork for a special concrete, which hardens at subzero temperatures. The final structure gets uncovered, while the ice is melting away in springtime.
