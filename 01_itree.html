CAN WE FORM TREES TO ROOT A NEW CITY?

// iTree //

MVRDV NEXT

In our everyday practice as architects, we are used to design our cities and building. Me make conceptual masterplans looking at the urban fabric, form and function. And in the end we just fill up the open space that is left with green structures. But what if we design those green structures first? What if the treescapes take form and we fill up the rest with urban fabric? Can we design the city like a forest and the forest like a city?
We made a conceptual approach towards that logic. After receiving a lot of data about the trees on a site in Amsterdam, we started filtering and designing the treescapes. We wanted to follow this inversion to provoke a discussion.
