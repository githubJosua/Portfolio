CAN WE AFFORD CURVED TERRACES?

// CHENGDU TIANFU SOWTWARE PARK //

MVRDV NEXT

One of the main features of the big tower is its double facade. On one side this is a way to react to the hot climate. On the other side it creates a nice intermediate space in between the facades. This space should be designed and used, which we wanted to accentuate through a contrast between the sleek glass facade and the organic stepped terraces.
To make those organic terraces economically feasible, we created modules of prefabricated concrete. With an avolution algorithm we managed to bring the number of individual panels down from approximately 400 to 40.
