Electronic Key Lock

• Often equipped with RFID Technology (Radio Frequency Identification)
• Capture the Radio Waves with a RFID reader (can be ordered online).
• Decode the sequence of numbers. Industrial locks have a frequency of 125Hz.
• Write the sequence onto a new RFID device (card, tag, ring).
• The new device can open the door just like the old one.
