E-Bike

• Determine if the bike uses RFID, NFC or Bluetooth (velib = NFC, nextbike = Bluetooth).
• In this case we have a bike that can be unlocked with a Bluetooth signal.
• Intercept the signal to gain the key or pairing credentials (Bluetooth sniffer).
• Use the spoofed Bluetooth signal to emulate a trusted device (e.g. phone).
• Unlock the e-bike with your phone.
